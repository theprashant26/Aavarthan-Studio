Our Story

In the National Capital Region (NCR), space is at a premium and aesthetics are constantly evolving. A perfect architectural and interior design solution exists but can be hard to find. Homeowners here are looking for luxurious yet functional living spaces. Business owners are looking for office or retail interiors and spaces that reflect their brand identity and add to the customer experience. Café owners dream of crafting inviting, stylish spaces that fit within a limited budget.

Uninspiring cookie-cutter designs, poor execution, and a lack of personalisation in architecture and interior work are big challenges here.

And that’s how Aavarthan Studio was born in 2022. After the pandemic, the architecture and interior design landscape in Delhi NCR suddenly shot up. We did not want to start just another architecture firm or interior company in Delhi NCR. We wanted to create something that can solve all these challenges and more.

The name ‘Aavarthan’ is derived from Sanskri. It means ‘revolution’ or ‘cycle’ - which symbolises our philosophy of continuous innovation. We believe that great designs are not only visually appealing but they also enhance the lifestyles of residents, evoke certain emotions in them, and improve their efficiency.

We Are Turning Dreams into Reality

At Aavarthan Studio, we understand that every space has a story to tell. Do you want your home to be your safe place - and a place where you can relax after a tiring and busy day? Or do you want an elite biophilic workspace where your employees feel more productive and engaged? Or do you dream of setting up a cosy coffee shop that draws in customers?

We have the expertise and creativity to bring your vision to life. Our architecture and interior work team blends modern trends with classic aesthetics, ensuring every design is both stylish and practical. We specialise in low-budget yet high-impact interior designs and architectural frameworks for a wide range of projects. From a coffee care interior to a co-working space, to some of the most luxurious apartments, we offer you architectural and interior work services that speak for themselves.

We craft interiors and architecture that are not just visually appealing but also enhance customer engagement - making every visit a memorable experience. We are good at infusing warmth and character into every space.

Our Journey is that of Passion and Innovation

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea. Interior design and architecture are more than aesthetics. We are in the business of creating spaces that inspire and endure.
We have worked with homeowners, corporate firms, retailers, and hospitality businesses - and have managed to impress them all with our expert design consultancy and complete turnkey solutions.

Unlike many architectural firms and interior design companies in Delhi, we do not offer you stale and overused designs. Every project we undertake is a new dream for us - and we take it as a challenge to prove ourselves. For us, architecture and interiors are living arts. They breathe, evolve, and resonate with those who experience them.
Aavarthan Studio is an architecture and interior firm in Delhi that understands the pulse of the city and the evolving needs of modern spaces. As one of the leading architecture and interior design firms in Delhi NCR, we push the boundaries of creativity, blending innovation with tradition to craft spaces that stand the test of time.

Our Reach

We proudly serve Greater Noida, Noida, Faridabad, Gurgaon, and South Delhi, bringing world-class architectural services and interior design solutions to diverse spaces.

Greater Noida

As an emerging business and residential hub, Greater Noida demands sleek, modern interiors. Our expertise in minimalist yet luxurious designs ensures that homes and offices here exude sophistication while remaining functional.

In commercial interiors, we have worked on smart storage solutions as well as contemporary office layouts that align with the fast-paced lifestyle of this area.

Noida

With its mix of corporate offices, upscale apartments, and trendy cafés, Noida is a design playground for Aavarthan Studio’s experts. We use biophilic design elements, smart home technology, and modular interiors in Noida to add to the urban living spaces and workspaces here.
If you’re looking for an interior for small cafe, contact us to see our amazing portfolio.

Faridabad

Faridabad is seeing industrial growth as well as residential expansion, People here want robust and aesthetically appealing interiors. We have done industrial-chic office designs and space-saving apartment interiors in the city.

Our Faridabad clients seem to prefer eco-friendly material choices that look both stylish and are more durable.

Gurgaon

A cosmopolitan hub with a luxury-driven clientele, Gurgaon demands cutting-edge design and build architecture as well as interior solutions. We specialise in high-end corporate office interiors, penthouse luxury designs, and bespoke residential projects.

Our focus on smart automation, minimalist luxury, and high-end finishes makes us a sought-after interior firm in Delhi NCR - especially in this area.

South Delhi

South Delhi is home to some of the most elegant villas, upscale apartments and marketplaces, and premium commercial spaces. We blend heritage-inspired aesthetics with contemporary elements here to create timeless interiors and architectural designs.

Whether you need a heritage-style home transformation or a modern retail space with artistic elements, we at Aavarthan Studio craft interiors and architectural solutions that define sophistication.
